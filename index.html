IT Student at Ndejje University

Hello! I'm Janet Awujia, a second-year Information Technology student at Ndejje University with a passion for creating innovative tech solutions and user-friendly applications.

I started my IT journey in high school and have since developed a strong foundation in programming, networking, and system administration. My goal is to become a web developer, Network Admin and work on projects that solve real-world problems in my community.

Once more i want to thank you all for making it to my website,my name is Awujia Janet a student at Ndejje university main campus Luweroo disstrict Ndejje town concil. All along my journey it have been God right from my primary upto my diploma level which am in now. I started my primary school from kizibu primary school from P.1 class upto P.6,then after due to some changes i finalized my P.7 from Hope nursery and primary school located at Kyankende parish in kiryandongo district.


After my primary level i went and joined Bulluge comprehesive secondary school where i finished my S.1 from because of fees issues i decided to come near home and i joined Bweyale s.s, where i started well my second level(S.2) to S.3 first term again i changed to Diika seed secondary school where i finished my O-level from.


By the grace of God my results came out when i passed i stayed at home for a year after in 2020 i joined Uganda cooperative College Kingumba (UCCK), just not even a month the outbreak of Corona in Uganda struck, it did not take long a lock down was placed mostly in public places and schools where among them we had to go back home. It was such a sad news for me i cried because i had planned my goals and time to achieve them. Corona brought suffering, death and putting things into stand still what kept me moving i had hope knowing that things gonna be well again. Whatever you put in prayers with faith God always do his things unexpectedly.


I appreciate God for making a way where there is no way and i got my certificate in 2023 at a field of Cooperatives and business Administration and people around me my family for unconditionally support, friends holding my back and i personally for being determined because if i could not be focused i could not make it.


Further more after my certificate due to financial constraints i remained home praying that one day i would resume my studies and upgrade my my paper in 2024 i did a driving school from Akomak driving school because i really loved seeinng women drive and i myself had the passion and the believe that i can make it like the rest and i took three month and things worked out for me


In august 2024, that is where a miracle happened and i resumed my studies where currently i am doing Diploma in Information Technology(IT) computer has been what my heart desired for long even though i did Cooperatives and business Administration, my heart was on Technology am really great full for whom i am becoming it was and is through patience, enduration, staying focused on what you want being and being determined


What i can tell my fellows out there always do not loose hope, stay humble, respect your self, parents, elders and even the young learn to have people with good motives around the ones who speaks about lighting the world and all in all put everything in prayers God comes in when you are defeated he knows the right time for everything to happen in your life, do not give up as i waited for long the way was open
